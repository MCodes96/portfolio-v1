02.About Me

Introduction

Hello! My name is Mikayil and I enjoy building good-looking and well-working software and websites. I got inspired to code by Apple products and especially Steve Jobs. I love the way Apple products just work seamlessly together and create a good user experience.


The computer Kid

Looking back it seems I should have gotten into coding much earlier in my life. I've always loved computers and wanted to know more about how they work. At first I got involved into computers through video games. I built my first gaming PC when I was 12 years old. It wasn't an impressive machine, but I was proud that I managed to build this working PC with a YouTube video. I became obsessed with optimizing, styling and customizing my system. I became the kid that everyone asked for computer advice and help.


First steps in programming

Fast forward a couple of years I wasn't playing as much video games anymore, but I became really obsessed with Apple's ecosystem. My first experiences in programming were made in Swift using an iPhone app and later finishing Swift Playgrounds Learn Swift course. At that point I knew a little bit about the Swift syntax and basic programming concepts, like If/Else, For & While Loops etc. I realized that I needed a solid foundation and I wanted a structured curriculum for that and that led me to FreeCodeCamp which is an amazing resource for beginners which I highly recommend as well as The Odin Project which is a bit harder to grasp at the beginning but prepares you right from the beginning to work like a real developer.


Time to get serious

After following these free curriculums and gaining some certifications in HTML, CSS and JavaScript but still not being able to fully grasp some concepts and connect the dots I decided to spend some money on my education and try another paid option which provided a "Full-Stack-Developer Career Path" with a very structured and well designed curriculum/path. Of course I am talking about Codecademy which has worked amazingly well for me. The way the course is structured with little guided projects mid-lesson and big portfolio projects with no guidance, videos, text led me to absorb the knowledge taught and gave me much better results.


Show more

This portfolio is a project for me. I've found this amazing design, that I wanted to recreate in my own code without looking at the original code. Learning Front- and Back-end development and combining these two is an amazing experience and I'm heavily invested in this journey, working on my skills every day.
